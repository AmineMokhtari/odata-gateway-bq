## 3. Core Narrative (Landing Page Version)

### You’re a Data Expert, Not a Ticket Waiter.
You know exactly what insights your business needs, but the data is locked in the BigQuery lakehouse. Today, your only path is a "SQL Tax": submitting a ticket, waiting days for an export, and struggling with broken CSVs that can't handle your complex, nested data. 

### Meet the "Safe Yes" for IT.
What if you could give your IT manager a reason to say "Yes" to live data access? Our gateway uses a **Trusted Subsystem** model. You sign in with your familiar work account (OIDC), and the gateway handles the rest. No GCP IAM permissions to manage, no Service Account keys to lose, and absolutely no "bill shock."

### Frictionless Discovery. Infinite Scale.
*   **Zero Drivers:** No ODBC or JDBC to install. Just a URL and your work login.
*   **Live Discovery:** New table created in GCP? It’s in your Excel Navigator instantly—even if it’s in a different region.
*   **Lossless Data:** We automatically cast BigQuery `RECORD` and `REPEATED` types to JSON, so you never lose fidelity in the transfer.
*   **Budget Guardrails:** Every query is scanned *before* it runs. If it exceeds your team's budget, it's blocked. You get the data; your company gets the peace of mind.

### Get Started for Free. Scale with Confidence.
The OData BigQuery Gateway is **free to download and deploy**. We believe data democratization shouldn't have a paywall. You get the full power of the gateway with no licensing fees. Need high-availability guidance, custom relationship manifests, or enterprise-grade SLAs? Our support team is here to help.

### Take It Now. Try It. Contact Us.
Stop waiting. Start discovering. Deploy the self-hosted OData BigQuery Gateway in your own GCP environment today. When you're ready to take it to production, contact us for an enterprise support plan.
